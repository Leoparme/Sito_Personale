A little bit about myself

Hey, I’m Leonardo - a UX/UI Designer that love build simple, intuitive interaction experiences that solve user problems in fun, aesthetically pleasing formats.

I was born in Rome, Italy. During my life, i was lucky enough to travel a lot since i was young. For this reason, the desire to learn foreign languages and look at the world from different perspectives, was born.
I lived in Spain and Toronto and after this experiences and meeting peoples from all over the world, I became fascinated with the way people think, feel, behave and learn from all their stories.

After 2 years dedicated to digital marketing and e-commerce, I found that my real passion was in the design side.
So I started and completed the full UX/UI Design Course of Start2Impact University, testing me with different design projects. Meanwhile, I always take a look at the web3 world and i help, through design, the first Italian community DAO 333.Builders.

In addition to design, I love hiking and practice physical training.
